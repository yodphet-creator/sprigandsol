About the Shop

Slow work.
Real ingredients.

SprigAndSol is a digital art shop built around one idea — that the things we cook with are worth looking at. Not styled. Not arranged. Just painted, quietly and carefully, the way a botanist might study them.

Each print begins as a detailed watercolor study. The brushwork is intentionally imperfect — visible strokes, translucent layers, paper grain showing through — because that's what makes it feel made by a person rather than generated by a machine.
